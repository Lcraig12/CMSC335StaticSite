Welcome To My Men's Club Lacrosse Site!


This is my site for Project 1!
I decided to write about my club team and some of the stuff we do at UMD!


The Team

At UMD, we have a solid team of student-athletes who play the sport for fun and competitively.

We have won back to back National Championships in 2023 and 2024!

Our Work

Our Lacrosse team is dedicated to helping our local communities through cleanups and fundraisers.

Our service activities include various events such as helping the UMD food pantry and cleaning up our local watershed.

My Highlights

During my time with the club, I have learned a lot of lessons in perseverance, social skills, and have made plenty of friends!
